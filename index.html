A Cafe Explorer's Global Adventure

Meet Angela, a travel enthusiast with a passion for cafes worldwide. She crisscrosses the globe, savoring unique brews and delectable treats, and she's eager to share her café discoveries with you. Angela's journey is a sensory delight, and her blog and social media provide a passport to experience the world's diverse café culture. Join her as she uncovers the soul of these cafes, connecting with local communities and sharing their stories. Interesting Cafes to Check Out

Countries


Go to Greem Cafe

From the interior to the tableware, Greem Café, with its unique charm, makes you feel like you stepped into a cartoon. By only painting the outlines of the whitewashed furniture in black, this unique cafe, with its 2D concept, creates the illusion of being in a cartoon. If you are looking for a cafe with a peculiar atmosphere, we recommend the Greem Café in Hongdae.



Go to Little Kook

Nestled in the heart of Athens, Greece, Little Kook is a whimsical, fairytale-themed café that will transport you to a magical world. With its enchanting decor, colorful desserts, and theatrical atmosphere, it's a must-visit destination for those seeking a unique and delightful cafe experience in the historic streets of Athens. Step into the enchanting realm of Little Kook and let your imagination run wild as you enjoy delicious treats in a fairytale setting.



Go to Konrad Cafe & Bar

Born as a vintage clothing shop & Cafè. Serving coffees & delicious cakes. Over the years is been the Cafè that has brought to luxembourg original events such as open mic, stand up comedy, theatre performances and much more. Konrad is the real outstanding original Cafè & bar. It has a unique vibe very rare to find somewhere else in the city!



Go to Blacklist Coffee Roasters

Blacklist Coffee Roasters, nestled in the heart of Australia, is a coffee lover's paradise. With a commitment to quality and sustainability, they meticulously source and roast their beans to perfection. Their cozy cafe is a haven for aficionados, offering a delightful array of specialty brews that showcase the finest flavors from around the world. Whether you're seeking a single-origin espresso or a rich, aromatic pour-over, Blacklist Coffee Roasters promises an unforgettable coffee experience Down Under.



Go to HOH Cafe

The "Hoh Cafe" in Taiwan is a hidden gem, nestled in the heart of bustling Taipei. Known for its enchanting ambiance and delectable Taiwanese tea and coffee blends, it's a haven for locals and travelers seeking a tranquil escape. With an array of exquisite teas and aromatic coffees, the Hoh Cafe offers an authentic taste of Taiwan's beverage culture. Don't miss the opportunity to immerse yourself in this cozy oasis and savor the flavors of Taiwan in a serene setting.



Go to Caffe Florian

Caffè Florian, located in the heart of Venice, Italy, is a historic gem that has been serving coffee and culture for over three centuries. Established in 1720, it's one of the world's oldest coffee houses and an iconic destination for travelers. With its opulent décor, live music, and exquisite coffee, Caffè Florian offers a timeless experience that transports visitors to an era of elegance and artistic inspiration. Don't miss the chance to sip a coffee at this Venetian institution, where history and ambiance blend in perfect harmony.
